Color Psychology

Color psychology is based on the idea that the colors we are exposed to can have an effect on our emotions and overall well being. Color therapy can be used as a means of healing. Exploring this may be helpful treatment for mental health and mood issues.

Red inspires passion, power, and energy

Red promotes energy, strength, motivation, confidence, will-power, courage, initiative, and attention to detail.

Good for depression, negativity, and shyness.

Avoid in anger and irritability.

Orange is used to heal the lungs and to increase energy levels.

Orange promotes a stimulated body and mind, self esteem, optimism, inner strength, creativity, self-expression, happiness, opens mind to new ideas and perspectives, and revitalisation.

Good for depression, negativity, trauma, stress, bereavement and loss.

Yellow is thought to stimulate the nerves and purify the body.

Yellow promotes uplifting, cleansing, self-respect, self-confidence, self-control, ability to rationalise and reason, contentment, and mental stimulation.

Good for depression, despair, fatigue, negativity, sensitivity to criticism, and lack of confidence.

Avoid in insomnia and hyperactivity.

Blue and Green create a calming atmosphere:

Green promotes balance, harmony, personal development, self-acceptance, compassion for self and others, and renewal.

Good for stress, anxiety, self-pity, and confusion.

Blue

Blue promotes calm, peace, relaxation, slowing down, steadying, self-expression, intuition, honesty, truth, and creativity.

Good for insomnia, stress, anxiety, overexcitement, and anger.

Purple enhances creativity and spirituality.

Purple promotes inspiration, imagination, empathy, sense of belonging, helping others, self-respect, and dignity

Good for stress, anxiety, obsessions, lack of confidence, low self-esteem, and depression.
