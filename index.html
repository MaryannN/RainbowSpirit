Rainbow Spirit

Welcome to Rainbow Spirit! Here you can take time to relax and explore while also learning about mental health.

Mental health is essential to our overall health and wellness but can often be overlooked in our day to day lives. Striving for good mental health is working towards a state of physiological and emotional well being. It is important for us to acknowledge and acquire information on ways to constantly improve our mental health status.
